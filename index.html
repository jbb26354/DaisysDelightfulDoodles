About Us

Golden Doodles

The Golden Doodle is a cross between a Poodle and a Golden Retriever. 

The resulting offspring is referred to as an  F1. 

The F1 is often bred back to a poodle to get a more hypo-allergenic coat.  The resulting offspring is an F1B.  When an F1B is bred back to a Poodle the resulting puppy is referred to as an F1B.B or sometimes a Multi-gen. 

Our Story

  

As a child, the dog of our house was usually some type of poodle. Poodles tend to  have a sweet temperament, are highly intelligent and very loyal. My life changed when we were blessed with a developmentally delayed and disabled child.  When my daughter, Rebecca turned 5, we started looking for a service dog for her. We raised over $5,000 and she received her dog Fayth when she was 6 years old. Fayth was a Golden Retriever . She was a very intelligent, loyal dog who developed an unbreakable bond with Rebecca. They got into mischief together and were hardly ever apart. Fayth helped Rebecca learn to communicate better and bridge the social gap between her and other people. Rebecca learned how to take care of Fayth and she was so proud of her wherever she went. She went everywhere with Rebecca- school, stores, restaurants, and even hospitals. Fayth passed away on Rebeccas 15th birthday. The next year we began looking for a dog to try and take her place. We were hoping for a non-shedding dog and thats when we heard about the Golden Doodle. The Golden Doodle comes from two unrelated breeds of dogs, the Golden Retriever and the Poodle.. Their first generation off spring puppies are referred to as hybrids. The percentage of hybrid dogs with genetic problems is much lower than purebred dogs because the gene pool is mixed.  My friend Sandi  was raising Golden Doodles and her puppies were adorable and getting raving reviews from all who had purchased them.  Her dog Zoey is an F1 and she was bred back to a Poodle. The result is  an F1B puppy. We put our deposit down and our pick of the litter was Daisy. From the moment we got her she was the most amazing puppy. She was calm, sweet , loving and so smart. We were all in love! We started training early and took her everywhere with us.. Everyone wanted to pet her soft fluffy fur and she wagged her tail wherever we went. She is a cuddler at home and loves all of the members of our family. Rebecca isnt seeing as well as she used to, so we spend a lot more time at home. Daisy is a comfort and more of a therapy dog to all of us. So, we decided that there should be more dogs just like her  and what better way than to have her own Delightful Designer Doodles. She is expecting her first litter of puppies on Christmas Day 2018. The father of her puppies, Regal is just as gentle and sweet as she is. Regal is a poodle and Daisy is an F1B. These puppies will be what some call Multigen. Others would call them F1B.B. They will be a great choice for anyone with allergy issues. There should be minimal to no shedding. These dogs will make excellent pets, service dogs or therapy dogs.  Daisys ultrasound showed that she is expecting at least 10 puppies. Two are already spoken for. This is how the story of Daisys Delightful Doodles Begins.
